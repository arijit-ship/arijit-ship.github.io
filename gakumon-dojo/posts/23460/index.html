    1. List of required documents

    The process of applying for the MEXT scholarship takes a while. The documents needed for the MEXT Scholarship application remain the same every year. This means you can begin gathering these documents before the guidelines are even published. In fact, in one situation, you might need to do this as you won‚Äôt have sufficient time later on.

    List of required documents

    # Document Comments
    1 Application form The form is exactly same for both the embassy & university tracks. You can download it from the embassy website during the application timeframe. If you are applying through the university track, the university is supposed to send you the application form
    2 Field of Study and Research Program Plan Again same form for both the tracks. Similar way you can obtain this too
    3 Letter of recommendation For the university track, you will require two letters of recommendation. The first should be from the dean or a higher authority at your current university, addressed to the president of the Japanese university you are applying to. The second letter should come from the hosting professor in Japan and should also be addressed to the same university president.
